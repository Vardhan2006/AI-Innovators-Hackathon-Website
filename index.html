About the Hackathon

Purpose

The Agentic AI Hackathon 2025 is a 48-hour online event by the AI Innovators Club. It challenges student teams to create lightweight AI agents for tasks like automation, content creation, or social impact, using accessible, low-cost tools. The event aims to foster innovation, collaboration, and learning in AI agent development.

Who Can Participate

Participation is exclusively open to currently enrolled students of the university. Whether you're a beginner exploring AI or someone experienced in building intelligent systems, this hackathon welcomes individuals and small teams (1‚Äì2 members) across all departments.

What to Expect

Participants will receive full support from the AI Innovators Club through WhatsApp groups and live sessions for smooth participation. Guidance will be provided from registration to final submission. The judging process will be fair, transparent, and unbiased, ensuring every team is evaluated equally.

Hackathon Themes

Productivity & Automation

Automate repetitive tasks: scheduling, email triage, content summarization, campus reminders, data entry helpers.

Creative & Content Generation

Generate or assist in producing text, images, audio, video, or marketing content; storytelling companions; design aids.

Social Impact & Accessibility

Tools for learning support, accessibility (assistive interfaces, voice helpers), health check reminders, environmental/campus reporting.
